This eye-catching Sterling 44 was built in 1986, completely refurbished in 2014 and has also undergone an extensive refit in 2018. The interiors and exquisite design offer unparalleled comfort. She has six beautifully lit cabins that welcome up to 12 guests as well and crew berths for 8 crew members. Powered by two Mitsubishi S12N - MTK engines, she reaches a maximum speed of 15 knots and has a transatlantic range. Head-turning features include her wide aft deck, outdoor jacuzzi and stabilizers for smooth cruising.
