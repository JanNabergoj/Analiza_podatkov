2008 Azimut 62S to sell

Azimut 62 S powered by 2x1015 Caterpillar C18 in shaft drive. Original photos. Elegant and still current lines. Never used for charter. Sold with European VAT paid.
2 cabin version with two bathrooms and crew cabin. Interior in satin wenge. For sale due to upgrade to smaller Fly.
April 2023: official Caterpillar service, replacement of sea cocks, replacement of zincs, antifouling on hull. May 2021: sampling of inverter engine oil for analysis; ZF 500 gearbox oil replacement, filter change (oil and diesel) and pre filters (diesel), turbo check and sea water pump replacement, belt replacement, air filter cleaning, cartridge filter replacement, air filter cleaning.
Azimut internal upholstery, living room and dinette floor woods and teak on the platform to be recovered.

Visit our website: www.valbroker.com
We can transport this vessel all over the world.
We speak English, French and German. Agency fees not included
