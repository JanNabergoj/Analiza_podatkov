1989 Gianetti Yacht 42 FLY to sell

Gianetti 42 Tunnel Drive: designed by Fulvio De Simoni, this motor yacht of almost thirteen meters offers good habitability and provides excellent speed performance. Boat with sober shapes, fast, stable and silent, with marine qualities and maneuverability of all respect thanks to the adoption of the tunnel drive. Well-maintained boat that was the subject of a deep refitting between 2018 and 2020.
