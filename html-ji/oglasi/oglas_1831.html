Comments

1992 Cantieri del Golfo Ipanema 15 fly, EUR 170.000,-

Splendid example of Ipanema 15 Fly signed by the hand of the architect Carlo Galeazzi and built by the Cantieri del Golfo (Italcraft).
The boat was refitted in 2021 and re-engined with a pair of 500hp IVECO 8460 SRM50 engines still under warranty.
These engines are considerably lighter and above all more fuel efficient than the GMs originally mounted, this has made it possible to reduce consumption from 180 lt/hr to 110 lt/hr and at the same time halve the noise.
