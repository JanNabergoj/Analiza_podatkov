2023 Sessa C54 to sell

The C54 Sport Coupé embodies the synthesis of design, comfort and performance that one would expect from a larger yacht. This 16 meters is able to express all the creative strength and competitiveness of the shipyard that, together with a generous distribution of spaces, has intelligently designed a "closed" salon so as to offer a welcoming and spacious environment. The immense sundeck and the 8-seater table contribute to making the main deck the real fulcrum of life on board, while, below deck, the 3 cabins, one of which is a master in the middle of the boat with a separate bathroom and the kitchen, spacious and habitable, ensure high levels of livability, as well as making every moment spent on board a real life experience.
