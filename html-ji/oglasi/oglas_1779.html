2018 Cranchi T 43 to sell

The T43 is an intelligent interpretation of the classic trawlers, an extremely efficient yacht with great functionality, designed to sail well and for a long time.
The comfort in navigation is another of the strengths of this yacht. Both indoors and outdoors, there are spaces with great attention to ergonomics and equipped with quality materials. The fittings are extremely accurate and free of smudges, as well as the assemblies and systems, easily inspected: nothing can disturb the relaxation during the cruise.
The design is characterized by linear windows and generous volumes in the forward sections determined by the choice of an almost vertical bow. The T43 Trawler makes the most of its almost 14 meters of hull length and 4.37 meters of maximum width.
