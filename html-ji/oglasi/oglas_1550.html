Sunseeker Camargue 50, EUR 330.000,-

This beautiful Sunseeker Camargue 50 has been maintained to highest standards and no expenses spared.

The Camargue 50 provides comfortable sociable cruising combined with excellent performance.

The relatively high hull volume makes it particularly stable and spacious within while direct shaft drives contribute to good manoeuvrability and performance with the responsiveness that inspires confidence. 

The Camargue 50 takes sports cruisers to new levels of excellence. With twin engines and direct shaft drives for directional stability and economical performance, she is capable of speeds of up to 35 knots and has a wide cruising range She offers a number of quality features, and includes exceptionally spacious accommodation, with having a spacious saloon and galley area, 2 large cabins, fore and aft, with en-suite bathrooms.
