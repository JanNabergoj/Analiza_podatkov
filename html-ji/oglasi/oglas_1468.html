2007 Mimì LIBECCIO 27, EUR 124.000,-

Cantieri Mimì proposes us with Libeccio 27, a planing gozzo of 9.60 meters overall, which was born with the aim of combining the long tradition of a shipyard that has been operating for 30 years in the nautical sector with the comfort and practicality provided by functional and innovative solutions.
A boat, therefore, built to meet the expectations of a demanding owner, who demands safety, speed and great livability. The generous use of precious woods embellishes the Libeccio 27, which is ideal both for daily outings, thanks to the large spaces of the cockpit and on the deck, and for longer cruises, since it offers considerable habitability and four comfortable beds. The boat from 2018 to today has not been used but completely refitted to "NEW".
